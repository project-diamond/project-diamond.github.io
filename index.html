Project Diamond
Digital Alternative to Monetary Denomination

We are a group of researchers exploring the possibility of a digital monetary system based on strong cryptography. We hope to build new cryptographic primitives, protocols and software that allows efficient and safe monetary transactions in the digital world. Stay tuned for more announcements here.
